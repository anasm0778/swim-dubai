HAVE ANY QUESTIONS? GET IN TOUCH WITH US

Whether you're looking to enroll your child in swimming lessons, book a private session at your home, or just want to learn more — we’re here to help!

SwimSafe Dubai is committed to providing safe, flexible, and personalized swimming programs for children aged 2 and above. Reach out to us today!

  • What is the minimum age to start swimming with SwimSafe?

    At SwimSafe Dubai, children can begin swimming lessons from the age of 2 years. Our certified instructors use fun and safe techniques tailored for toddlers.

  • Do you offer home or community pool training?

    Yes, we provide private swimming lessons at your home or community pool across Dubai. Our coaches bring all necessary equipment and follow strict safety protocols.

  • What safety measures are in place during lessons?

    Safety is our top priority. All our instructors are certified in CPR and first aid, and we maintain a low student-to-instructor ratio to ensure maximum supervision.

  • Can I reschedule or cancel a session?

    Absolutely. We offer flexible scheduling. You can reschedule a session up to 24 hours in advance without any cancellation charges.
