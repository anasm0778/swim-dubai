LEARN TO SWIM – FOR EVERY AGE

Our SwimSafe Levels

At SwimSafe, we offer a structured and progressive swim program tailored for every swimmer—from beginners to advanced. Whether you're building water confidence or preparing for swim meets, we’ve got you covered.

  • AQUA1 – Beginner Water Confidence

    Perfect for new swimmers. Focus on water confidence, safety, and basic movement with support in a fun and secure environment.

  • AQUA3 – Skill Development

    Ideal for swimmers ready to learn strokes, improve breath control, and build endurance with growing independence in the water.

  • AQUA4 – Technique & Endurance

    Focus on refining strokes, increasing swim stamina, and mastering deeper water skills for confident long-distance swimming.

  • AQUA5 – Stroke Mastery

    Master all four strokes, ensure full water safety, and prepare for advanced squad or competitive swim training with technical precision.

  • Silver Level – Technique Refinement

    Advanced swimmers focus on refining stroke efficiency, building endurance, and preparing for Gold Level or competitive meets.
